Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our pricing and features.

Can I change my plan anytime?

Yes! You can upgrade or downgrade your plan at any time. Changes take effect immediately, and we'll prorate any billing differences.

Is there a free trial for Premium?

Absolutely! We offer a 14-day free trial for our Premium plan. No credit card required to start your trial.

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, and bank transfers for Enterprise plans.

Can I cancel my subscription anytime?

Yes, you can cancel your subscription at any time. Your account will remain active until the end of your current billing period.

Do you offer discounts for annual billing?

Yes! Save 20% when you choose annual billing for Premium and Enterprise plans. The discount is automatically applied at checkout.

Is my financial data secure?

Absolutely. We use bank-level 256-bit SSL encryption, regular security audits, and comply with GDPR and other privacy regulations.
